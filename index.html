Hi, I'm Ahmed Abdirahim Osman

I am a passionate graphic designer and freelancer with a keen eye for detail and a commitment to delivering high-quality work. My journey began with a deep interest in visual arts and technology, and has since evolved into a rewarding career.

I specialize in creating visually compelling designs, from logos and branding materials to motion graphics and web interfaces. My expertise spans various design tools and software, including Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects) and Figma.

In addition to my design skills, I have experience in web development, utilizing technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with frameworks such as Tailwind CSS and React. I am also proficient in backend development with Node.js and database management with MongoDB.
